# Pristine PM — Scope Definition

Last updated: 2026-04-03

## What this repo owns
pristine-pm is the **single operating hub** for Pristine Clean Club. Everything needed to run the business lives here.

## Layer structure

| Layer | Purpose | Files |
|-------|---------|-------|
| Governing | Rules, doctrine, standards | `playbooks/07-Pristine-Doctrine-System.md`, `playbooks/01-Identity-and-Standards.md` |
| Truth | Current facts about the business | `active/pristine/CONTEXT.md`, `active/pristine/FORMULATION.md`, `active/pristine/PRODUCT.md`, `legal/`, `contacts/` |
| Thinking | Frameworks for decisions, communication, relationships | `playbooks/02-06`, `modules/` |
| Execution | Active work, status, decisions, blockers | `active/pristine/STATUS.md`, `active/pristine/DECISIONS.md`, `active/pristine/ACTION_PLAN.md`, `active/pristine/OPEN_QUESTIONS.md`, `active/pristine/RISKS.md` |

## What stays in ColeOS (not here)
- Personal founder identity and non-Pristine ventures
- Cross-venture contacts and relationships
- Personal legal/financial documents unrelated to Pristine

## What does NOT belong here
- Experimental or unvalidated content (keep in session notes or OPEN_QUESTIONS.md)
- Duplicate summaries of content already in canonical files
- Artifacts (design files, images, exports) — store in Google Drive or equivalent
