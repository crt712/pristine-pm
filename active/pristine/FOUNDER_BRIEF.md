## Strategic Position

- **Category:** Premium preservation fabric system—not “clean / eco / natural / green” as the competitive frame.
- **Compete in:** Fabric preservation through disciplined design.
- **Ship rule:** If it does not reinforce preservation authority, it does not ship.
- **Doctrine alignment:** Decision standards and capital order in `playbooks/07-Pristine-Doctrine-System.md` (performance integrity, cohesion, margin, authority tone, scalability).

## Brand Positioning

- **Frame:** Premium eco-smart fabric care; **identity:** modern preservation authority.
- **Not:** Budget green detergent, legacy chemical brand, traditional softener, wellness gimmick.
- **Are:** Chemistry-forward sustainability, fabric longevity, performance formulation, system architecture, premium modern aesthetic.
- **Lines:** Clean Without Compromise; **primary message:** Preserve what you invest in. **System thesis:** Performance compounds across touchpoints.

## Product System

- **Phase 1 architecture (cheat sheet):** Clean → Treat → Finish (preservation protocol, not arbitrary merchandising). **Doctrine ladder:** Reset → Fortify → Refine—map explicitly for external copy.
- **SKUs (planning):** Ultra-concentrated liquid detergent (QCMC, revenue anchor); rinse-stage treatment (primary QCMC deposition); dryer-stage finisher (heat-activated finish). Targets, COGS assumptions, attach rates: see cheat sheet §VII–IX.
- **Technical ground truth:** Formulation/CMC state in `FORMULATION.md` (solubility/stability gates, FCS workstream).

## Science / Claims Guardrails

- QCMC = Quaternized Carboxymethyl Chitosan; mechanism framing: binding, soil release, redeposition, odor moderation, softness, cumulative benefit—**not** health outcomes.
- **No:** antimicrobial, germ-kill, EPA pesticide, medical, or unapproved certification claims.
- **R&D discipline:** Performance and regulatory/counsel review before marketing claims (`FORMULATION.md` + cheat sheet §VIII).
