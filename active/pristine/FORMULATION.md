## MATERIAL SPECIFICATIONS — CHITOSAN DERIVATIVES (QCMC / CMCS)

Supplier docs only (Promecens COA/MSDS). Use lot-specific COA for release; this block is operator reference, not a spec sheet.

### QCMC — Summary

- **Identity:** Quaternary Carboxymethyl Chitosan (QCMC); CAS **83512-85-0**; MW (avg. stated) **290.75 g·mol⁻¹** (supplier).
- **Manufacturer:** Promecens Entosystems Private Limited.
- **Example batch (COA):** QCMC/01/2025; mfg **Oct 2025**; exp **Sep 2028**.
- **Formulation-relevant:** Fully water soluble; degree of quaternization **~0.53**; purity **~99.28%** dry basis; **1%** viscosity **~1.09 cS** at 25°C; **1%** pH reported **3.03** and **6.8** (dual readings on COA); insolubles **0.04%**.
- **Stability / handling:** Hygroscopic; avoid humidity and heat; stable under ambient storage per MSDS; incompatible with strong oxidizers, strong acids, strong bases.
- **Safety / positioning signal:** Not classified hazardous under REACH/CLP in MSDS; mild mechanical irritation from dust; oral LD50 **>5000 mg/kg** (rat); biodegradable; low environmental persistence per MSDS.
- **Storage / shelf life:** **~3 years** sealed, dry, **<30°C** (MSDS).

### CMCS — Summary

- **Identity:** Carboxymethyl Chitosan (CMCS / Carboxymethylated Chitosan RD); CAS **83512-85-0**; MW **~25 kDa** (COA).
- **Manufacturer:** Promecens Entosystems Private Limited.
- **Example batch (COA):** CMCRD/03/2026, lot CMCRD-0302-01; mfg **Mar 2026**; exp **Feb 2028**.
- **Formulation-relevant:** Water soluble per spec with **1–2 h** magnetic stir at RT, **do not heat**; degree of deacetylation **~98.67%**; purity **~99.13%**; **1%** viscosity **~6.57 cSt** at 30°C; **1%** pH **~4.43**; insolubles **0.01%**.
- **Stability / handling:** Avoid moisture and direct sunlight (MSDS); stable under recommended storage; incompatible with strong oxidizers.
- **Safety / positioning signal:** Not classified hazardous (REACH) in MSDS; mild mechanical irritation from dust; oral LD50 **>5000 mg/kg** (rat); biodegradable per MSDS.
- **Storage / shelf life:** **2–5 years** under recommended storage; dry, cool, sealed, **<30°C** (MSDS).

### Formulation Implications (Pristine Context)

- **QCMC:** High solubility and low viscosity suit liquids; permanent cationic charge may conflict with enzymes or anionic surfactants (validate in matrix).
- **CMCS:** Often easier compatibility story than QCMC for some systems, but **dissolution time and no-heat** rule add process risk on the line.
- **Both:** Low acute tox and biodegradable profile supports premium "gentler chemistry" framing; **claims need regulatory and counsel review**.
- **Both:** Moisture-sensitive powders: packaging integrity and warehouse/plant humidity control matter.
- **Execution bottleneck:** **Solubility** was the prior hard gate; the **new CMC grade** is the active remediation. **Controlled dissolution + finished-product stability** under wash and manufacturing conditions remain the gating technical work until readouts land.
