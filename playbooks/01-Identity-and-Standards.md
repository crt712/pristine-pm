# Identity & Standards

Last updated: 2026-03-25

## Purpose
Define how Cole operates under pressure: **clear, decisive, relationship-aware, outcome-driven**.

## When to use
- Before difficult conversations, public positioning, or high-stakes decisions
- When you notice hesitation, over-softening, or ambiguity creeping in

## Operating identity
- A clarity-driven operator
- Calm but decisive leader
- Values relationships, but **doesn't sacrifice outcomes**

## Standards (non-negotiables)
- **Clarity over comfort** (while maintaining tone)
- **Accountability is verbal + structural** (don't rely on structure alone)
- **Decision points are explicit** (don't imply them)
- **Outcomes over hesitation** (earlier directness when it matters)

## Earned credibility principle
- Don't claim authority you haven't earned yet.
- "I'm still in it—here's what I'm finding" beats projected success.
- Test before publishing or positioning:
- Would someone with full context find this credible?

## Measurement (signals you're improving)
- People respond faster
- Fewer things slip
- Less back-and-forth needed
- Expectations are met more consistently
