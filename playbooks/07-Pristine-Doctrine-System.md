# Pristine Doctrine System

Last updated: 2026-03-28

## Purpose
Canonical doctrine for Pristine decision standards.

## FOUNDERS_CHEAT_SHEET.md — promotion bar
`active/pristine/FOUNDERS_CHEAT_SHEET.md` is a high-signal, canonical snapshot for operators and UI. Add or change content **only** when **all** of the following are true:

- **Stable** — not expected to change frequently
- **High-signal** — defines company, product, positioning, or structure
- **Resolved** — not in progress, not actively debated, not conditional on unset decisions
- **Operator- or UI-relevant** — belongs in a durable reference or on-screen founder view

**Do not** put the following in the cheat sheet (keep them in `STATUS.md`, `CONTEXT.md`, `OPEN_QUESTIONS.md`, `FORMULATION.md`, `RISKS.md`, or session notes instead):

- Experiments, partial thinking, or validation-stage content
- Open questions, temporary assumptions, or "working" figures not yet treated as committed

## PRODUCT.md — canonical product / SKU definitions
`active/pristine/PRODUCT.md` is the canonical place for **product lineup, internal SKU identifiers, fragrance names, and sellable variants**. Planning economics and founder-facing snapshot stay in `FOUNDERS_CHEAT_SHEET.md`; chemistry and materials stay in `FORMULATION.md`. Do not treat hypothetical examples elsewhere in the repo as product truth until reflected in `PRODUCT.md`.

## Core strategic position
- Pristine competes in **fabric preservation through disciplined design**.
- Preservation is the mission; cleaning is a function.
- System logic is non-negotiable: **Reset -> Fortify -> Refine**.
- Consumer-facing Phase 1 system phrasing (**Clean → Treat → Finish**) lives in `active/pristine/FOUNDERS_CHEAT_SHEET.md`; keep preservation-first meaning aligned when mapping to the doctrine ladder.
- Consumer evidence layer (Mar 2026 survey) is summarized in `active/pristine/CONTEXT.md`; doctrine does not change, but **do not** contradict preservation-first positioning without an explicit doctrine revision.

## Decision standard (Pristine-specific)
All meaningful decisions must protect:
- Performance integrity
- System cohesion
- Margin durability
- Authority tone
- Long-term scalability

If any of these weaken, pause or reject.

## Capital discipline
- Capital follows durability, not momentum.
- Spend order: performance integrity -> margin protection -> controlled signal -> amplification.
- No expansion before stable product, stable margin, and defensible claims.

## Operating cadence
- Use canonical cadence module: `modules/pristine-operating-cadence.md`
- Apply the weekly, monthly, and quarterly reviews defined there.

## Guardrails
- No regulatory-overreaching claims (especially antimicrobial language without required pathway).
- No feature/sku expansion that fragments system architecture.
- No growth theatrics that dilute authority.

## Execution source of truth
- Active decisions and current risk posture live in:
- `active/pristine/CONTEXT.md`
- `active/pristine/STATUS.md`
